RESUME PLACEHOLDER

This is a placeholder file for your resume. 

To complete your portfolio setup:

1. Create a professional PDF resume
2. Name it "resume.pdf"
3. Place it in the public/ directory
4. The download button in the Resume section will automatically work

Your resume should include:
- Contact information
- Professional summary
- Work experience
- Education
- Skills (especially MERN stack)
- Projects
- Certifications

Make sure your resume is:
- ATS-friendly (Applicant Tracking System)
- Well-formatted and professional
- Tailored for remote/full-stack developer positions
- Updated with your latest experience and skills

The portfolio is designed to highlight your MERN stack expertise and remote work readiness!
